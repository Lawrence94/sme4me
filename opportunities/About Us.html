About Blonde

Blonde is a full service digital agency with 86 people in Edinburgh and London. It provides ingenious digital solutions to commercial problems.

We use code, content, conversation and creativity to great effect.

Ingenuity is about elegant (often non-obvious) strategies, services, experiences and ideas.

More often than not ingenious solutions are the direct result of a well defined problem. For this reason we place great emphasis on the definition of purpose for every project.

A well articulated, commercially valuable sense of purpose enables ingenious thinking and sets the agenda for rigorous evaluation of success.

We like our problems upstream and strategic. The more fundamental the problem, the easier it is to assign real commercial value to the solution.

Blonde is strong in the following areas, across all platforms and devices:

  • Complex, multi-stakeholder projects.
  • User Experience design and optimisation.
  • Digital design.
  • Commercial, purposeful social media.
  • Content strategy.
  • Content creation.
  • Visibility (getting stuff seen by the right people through the best possible blend of paid and organic means).
  • Consultancy (purposeful, ingenious strategies for the use of technology and digital platforms).
  • Service design.
